📋 Judging Criteria & Scoring Rubric

1. Clarity of Problem Understanding
Weight: 15%
Did the team clearly define the problem? Specific, measurable, relevant?
2. Quality of AI Use
Weight: 20%
Is AI genuinely useful, technically sound, appropriately integrated? Beyond superficial use?
3. Business Value
Weight: 15%
Does the solution create real business/societal value? Clear value proposition and target market?
4. Feasibility
Weight: 10%
Can this realistically be implemented? Technical, operational, and resource feasibility.
5. Responsible AI & Risk Awareness
Weight: 15%
Did they identify ethical risks, data privacy, bias, governance issues? Mitigation strategies?
6. Implementation Roadmap
Weight: 10%
Credible plan to production? Timeline, milestones, dependencies, next steps?
7. Quality of Final Presentation
Weight: 15%
Was the pitch clear, compelling, well-structured? Effective communication within time constraints?
